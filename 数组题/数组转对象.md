```js
let arr=[
  {user:"123"},
  {Cause:"22"},
  {EnterFactoryTime:"33"},
  {OutFactoryTime:"44"},
  {VehicleGrade:"55"},
  {IncomingInspection:"66"},
  {Admission:"77"}
];
```
转换为
```js
  let obj={
     user:"123",
     Cause:"22",
     EnterFactoryTime:"33",
     OutFactoryTime:"44",
     VehicleGrade:"55",
     IncomingInspection:"66",
     Admission:"77"
 };
```

方法：

`var result= arr.reduce((item1,item2)=>Object.assign(item1,item2));`

or

`Object.assign(...arr)`
